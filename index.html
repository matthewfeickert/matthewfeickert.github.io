About

I am a Ph.D. candiate in experimental high energy particle physics at Southern Methodist University working on the ATLAS experiment at CERN.

My research is focused on measurements of the Higgs bosons's couplings to heavy flavor quarks and searches for exotic physics beyond the standard model. Given my work I have a particular interest in:

  • statistical techniques for physics
  • machine learning
  • scientific applications of Python
  • reproducible data analysis pipelines
